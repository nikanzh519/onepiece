Cultural Impact

One Piece has influenced our culture in different aspects:

  • One piece is expansive and culturally diverse, it takes many cultures and makes it into one big text making it fun to read and very creative/interesting
  • One peices cultural impact extends far beyond Japan and anime making video games to even theme parks about one peice
  • One piece became so engaging to so many cultures and religions that in the series it has been translated to numerous languages, reaching audience world wide
