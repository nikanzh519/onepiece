Introduction

One Piece is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda. It follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy and his pirate crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, as they search for the legendary treasure known as the "One Piece" to become the Pirate King.

  • First published: July 22, 1997
  • Author: Eiichiro Oda
  • Guinness World Record: Most copies published for the same comic book series by a single author
